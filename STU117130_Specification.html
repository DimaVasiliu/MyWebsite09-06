Objective/Goals:

The objective of this website is to provide a comprehensive guide for travelers who are looking to plan their next holiday. The goals are to offer a wide range of holiday packages, attract potential customers, and provide an easy and convenient way for users to book their holiday.

Key audience(s):

The key audience for this website includes individuals and families who are interested in traveling for leisure. The target audience is primarily adults between the ages of 25 and 55 who have disposable income and are looking for unique and memorable travel experiences.


Site structure:

  • Home page:
    • Introduction to the website and a showcase of the latest travel packages.

  • Destinations page:
    • A list of popular holiday destinations and the various holiday packages available for each location.

  • About Us page:
    • Information about the website, and its commitment to providing exceptional travel experiences.

  • Reviews page with a form:
    • Reviews and testimonials from previous customers who have booked holidays through the website, and a feedback form.

  • Contact Us page:
    • A contact form for users to submit inquiries, as well as contact information for the company.

Functional specification:

The website should have a search function that allows users to search for travel packages by destination, price range, and travel dates. The website should also have an easy-to-use booking system that allows users to select their desired holiday package and complete the booking process online. The website should be optimized for mobile devices to ensure a seamless user experience across all devices.

Competitor Analysis:

The website should be designed with the competition in mind, and should offer unique features and benefits that set it apart from other travel booking websites. The competition includes websites such as Expedia, Travelocity, and TripAdvisor. The website should offer competitive pricing, a wide range of travel packages, and exceptional customer service to attract and retain customers.
